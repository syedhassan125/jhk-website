How It All
Began

The origins of JHK Group date back to 1920, when our founder, Abdul Hakim Khan, established the family's presence in the business landscape of the region.

Over successive decades, the family built a strong reputation within the construction sector, delivering significant infrastructure and development projects across Pakistan — rooted in integrity, reliability, and operational excellence.

Building upon this heritage, JHK Construction Company was formally established in 2009. Through disciplined execution and strong stakeholder relationships, JHK has grown into a trusted partner for government departments and international development institutions.
